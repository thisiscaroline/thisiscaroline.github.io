Soft skills

Also known as "people skills," these are characteristics that one has absolutely no choice but to develop when one picks up a customer-friendly job, such as a Starbucks barista at a busy university campus. Because you can't serve hundreds of caffeine-starved their morning joe without at least a little compassion.
